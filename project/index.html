Spectral Compressive Imaging via Unmixing-driven Subspace Diffusion Refinement
Haijin Zeng*, Benteng Sun*, Yongyong Chen, Jingyong Su, Yong Xu
ICLR 2025 Spotlight · Top 4.79%
A novel framework for spectral compressive imaging (SCI) reconstruction that leverages diffusion models to recover high-frequency details from a single compressed measurement, addressing the challenges of limited MSI training data and high computational demands.
Keywords: Spectral Imaging, Diffusion Models, Image Reconstruction
